CLOTHES



Traditional male attire in Maharashtra includes the dhoti, also known as Dhotar, and pheta, while a choli and nine-yard saree locally known as Nauwari saree or Lugda is for women. Traditional attire is becoming rarer with trousers and shirts for males and five yard saree or salawar khameej for females as the popular replacements. The traditional attire is increasingly worn by Maharashtrians only during special occasions and festivals.
